Movie Project

Context: The Bear's Eating Contest is the title of the movie I made with my partner and the prompt was to create a fake movie webpage with my partner.

Content: Story is about two bears having an eating contest with each other and the bigger bear wins. But then the big bear realizes that winning is not always better.

Challenges: Some challenges I have making this movie project was trying to think of what to write about. I was in charge of writing the story so thinking about what to write was a huge challenge as it was one of the most important parts that is needed in the project. But overall the project was really fun to make.

Take aways: Some takeaways I have is to communicate. Communication is key, without communication, this project wouldn't have been made. Another take away I have is that if u dont know how to do css or html, you should be the one doing it. By this you will get better and learn.

Next step: For the next step something I would do is to try and experience a little more with the movie. I would like to test out more background colors and fonts, also even the writing of the story. This may help the movie become better as I am able to see the full potential of what I can do for this movie project.
