SEP11 Freedom Project

Context

I am a student at HSTAT in the Software Engineering Program. The "Freedom Project" for SEP11 is a year-long project all about making something using JavaScript along with a third-party JS tool. For my project, I chose to independently study P5play in order to help me make my Budgetting App.

Content

For this project we had to create a game or app of our choice using Java script and the tool we choose. So for me I choose the tool p5Play and I incorprated it in my Budgetting App as seen through the background. So I choose to make a Budgetting App becuase a lot of people now a days don't really know how to spend their money wisely. Through this Budgetting App people will be more financially organize and know how to manage their money more respobisibly.

Challenges

One challenge I faced while creating this app was getting the background animation to work properly. No matter what I tried in either p5.js or p5play, the background would not load correctly. This was super frustrating as the background animation was an important part that makes the app less boring and plain. I spent time testing different solutions and checking my code for errors. Eventually, I used Claude AI as a tool to help me identify the error and guide me through fixing it. After making the necessary changes, I was able to get the background animation working.

Takeaway

Some takeaways that I had while creating this project was time management and the importance of debugging and using available resources to solve coding problems. For time management I struggled alittle with following the timeline I created as I had loads of other assignment and homework to do which led me to procrassionate alittle. Next time I will do a better job at making sure I actually follow the timeline I created rather than just not using it as a whole. The imporatance of using available recources is very beneficial as when you are stuck in this difficult situation with no one around, just know that there will always be tools that can help you with debugging.
