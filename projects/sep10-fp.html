SEP10 Freedom Project

I am a student at HSTAT in the Software Engineering Program. The "Freedom Project" for SEP10 is a year-long project all about making a website that informs the viewer of the current and future innovations in the topic of my choosing through using HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Github, and I also chose to independently study Animate.CSS in order to help me make my website better.

For my project, I chose the topic of Music Production because I am curious by how all these great music I listen to get created. In this freedom project we have to create an innovation in which I made a possible innovation called the Mood Adapt Music Player which is a cool device which can give you a personalized music recomendation based on your current mood and speech. So in order to create this website I used HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and Github. I kept all my notes of my progress of studying this tool in my learning log.
